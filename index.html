About

Shri Jai Narayan Chouksey, born in Bilaspur (C.G.), completed his post-graduation from Robertson College, Jabalpur. In 1994, he founded the Lakshmi Narain College of Technology (LNCT), Bhopal under the H.K. Kalchuri Education Trust.

Since its inception, the LNCT Group has grown into one of the largest and most preferred educational groups in India, offering quality education in Engineering, Management, Pharmacy, and Medical fields. Many of its engineering programs are NBA accredited and the group is ISO-9001 certified.

With the motto "Working Towards Being the Best," LNCT has produced a vast network of successful alumni working globally in leading multinational companies.

The LNCT campus in Bhopal spans about 50 acres, featuring academic and research buildings, digital classrooms, modern labs, Wi-Fi, a central library, hostels, mess, workshops, sports grounds, and an auditorium—providing a comprehensive environment for holistic learning.
